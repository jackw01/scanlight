# parts list

## 3d printing note
Printing orientation for all parts should be obvious based on the design. Print all parts in black ABS or ASA, using a 0.4mm or 0.6mm nozzle with 4 or 3 perimeters, respectively, 0.2mm layer height, and 30% infill. Fuzzy skin may be used on external contours only (**NOT** the painted interior surfaces of the diffuser box.) The "Support on Build Plate Only" or equivalent option should be used since several parts have counterbore holes.

* 20x20mm T-slot aluminum extrusion at least 350mm long, depending on close focusing ability of your lens
* 3D printed diffuser mount
* 3D printed camera clamp cleat
* 3D printed camera clamp cam
* 3D printed camera clamp slider
* 3D printed M3x1mm spacer (quantity = 4 * (*camera_height* - 37))

* 2x M3x8 socket head cap screw
